1. PV Name Formats

ESS EPICS PV names follow one of four formats. The colon (:) separates the three main parts.

Full device PV (most common)

System-Subsystem:Discipline-Device-Index:Property

Example: DTL-010:EMR-TT-001:Temperature

Without subsystem

System:Discipline-Device-Index:Property

Example: ISrc:Vac-VG-001:Pressure

High-level system PV

System::Property   or   System-Subsystem::Property

Example: Tgt::PowerConsumption or DTL-010::Temperature
